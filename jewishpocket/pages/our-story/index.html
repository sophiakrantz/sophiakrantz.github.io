    Information

The purpose of this website is to create a resource for critically examining anti-Semitic writing and rhetoric over time, as a means of understanding how it developed and has played a role in general society and popular culture. An additional goal of this website is to understand how the common themes of alienating and villainizing a minority group are played out on different people even to this day. I hope that in reading and analyzing some of the truth and the lies behind these writings people will be able to understand patterns in hate speech and be able to identify and stand up against them.
