Sophia Krantz
I'm a New Jersey based multimedia artist with one foot in the digital and the other in the analog. I have an AAS in Computer Animation and a BFA in Design but there's little in the visual arts that I dislike delving into. My work shows the culmination of my personal experiences with my perception of the outside world and I am determined to bring into understanding the world as I see it.
