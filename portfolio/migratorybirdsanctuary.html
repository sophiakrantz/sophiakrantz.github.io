Migratory Bird Sanctuary

Brand identity

The Migratory Bird Sanctuary is a brand identity designed for the Yellow Sea / Bohai Gulf of China, a UNESCO world heritage site. The location, encompassing dozens of kilometers of wetlands, serves as a breeding ground for birds, both endangered and otherwise, passing through the area.

The brand identity for the site utilizes the silhouettes of birds native to the area and draws on China's national colors of red and yellow for its palette.
